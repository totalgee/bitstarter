Papuga is a mobile, online game in which players must master the art of imitation. The game will feature ongoing challenges, with recordings of celebrities ("Go ahead, make my day!") and historical figures ("This was their Finest Hour") to mimic. Players can also create private challenges to share with their own circle, allowing for playful competition with friends and family ("No dessert 'til you finish your greens, mister!").

Players will accumulate their scores and ratings, on their way to becoming...King Papuga.

The main interface will be a mobile app, for Android or iOS (both, if we receive sufficient funding). Players will be able to browse for challenges or create new ones, upload or listen to reference tracks, then record and submit their own impersonation.

An audio analysis algorithm will automatically assign a score to each submission, based on how closely it matches the reference. Additionally, players will be able to assign scores to submitted recordings. Although the "objective" analysis will favour the accuracy of impersonation, human players may value other attributes, such as humour.
